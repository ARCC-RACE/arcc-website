About Us

Michael Equi, our founder, started ARCC in 2018 with the dream of educatng people about Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology: “I and many others see AIas the technological solution to many of today’s most baffling problems. From climate change to immigration and improving the overall quality of life, AI has its set of benefits that it promises if handled correctly. In the meantime, it is important to understand that technology is what allows humanity to scale and grow either in population or in quality of life. It is important therefore that we constantly push the boundaries of what we can do. Therefore we need to make sure that as a community we learn, train, and exercise our ability to innovate which is the key reason for ARCC’s founding.” To improve our chances of a successful human society merged with AI, we need to create organizations that foster the greatest levels of collective human intelligence. By studying and contributing to the field of AI whether as a technical developer, team leader, or AI philosopher we are helping humanity take a course on what could be one of the biggest revolutions. ARCC and R.A.C.E. will add to the pool of collective intelligence available to humanity in solving the challenges of AI. The goal is that ARCC will serve as a source of inspiration and education for our youngest generation. By tying in DeepRacer and NVIDIA Jetson Car to AI education, ARCC hopes to attract a wider audience and community that share in the passion for AI, robotics, and the prospects of a better future for humanity.
